Garlic Topped Chips

Grab some fries, toss them with this garlic/olive oil for easy, but delicious garlic fries. But make sure you are surrounded by those who will love you no matter how strong your garlic breath is!

Burger King Replica Zinger

Heat oil in a pan or pot to 350F. Place the mince into the oil and let it fry for 6 minutes, or until golden and crispy. Simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, for 7 minutes. Uncover and simmer for 3 minutes until mixture thickens. Season.

Chicken Maryland

Coriander root & stem adds great flavour to the beans. Wash well to remove any dirt. Leftover smoky barbecue marinade is great on pizzas. Spread it over the base and top with cheese, chicken and roasted capsicum strips before baking.
